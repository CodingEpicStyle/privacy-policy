Privacy Policy for THANKSMOM
Last updated: January 4, 2026
THANKSMOM is an experimental mobile game developed for internal research purposes. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.
Information We Collect
Device Identifier
We generate a random identifier (UUID) stored locally on your device. This identifier:

Is created by our app, not derived from hardware or advertising IDs
Persists across sessions to measure retention patterns
Cannot identify you personally
Can be deleted upon request

Gameplay Behavior Data
We collect anonymous behavioral data to analyze game design, including:

Response timing (how quickly you interact with game elements)
Session duration and frequency
Score and performance patterns
In-game choices and actions
Interruption tolerance metrics (response to loading screens)

What We Do NOT Collect

Name, email, or contact information
Location data
Device hardware identifiers (IDFA, GAID, Android ID)
IP addresses
Photos, files, or other personal content
Data from other apps

How We Use Information
All collected data is used solely for internal research:

Analyzing and improving game design
Understanding player engagement patterns
Measuring tolerance for interruptions
Academic research on user behavior

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your data with any third parties.
Data Security
All data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption. Data is stored locally on your device and in secure internal systems accessible only to the research team.
Data Retention
Behavioral data is retained for the duration of the research study. Local data persists on your device until you uninstall the app or request deletion.
Data Deletion
You may request deletion of your data by contacting:
Email: duboishdesigns@gmail.com
Since we use a self-generated identifier rather than personal information, please include approximate install date or any session details that help us locate your data.
Children's Privacy
THANKSMOM is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through our app, contact us for deletion.
Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy as the research evolves. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
Contact
